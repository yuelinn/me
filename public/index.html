Hi, I'm Yue Linn.
Robotics Researcher

I believe intelligent robots are pivotal in solving global problems. I am a Master of Engineering graduate with experience in deep learning, software engineering, mechanical design and fabrication, and electronics.

I am currently running my own research on using intelligent robots to solve real-world problems. My main focus is in controls and perception for swarm robots in field applications such as agriculture.

Projects

The PJ 29 Lab

This garage lab research and develops intelligent robotics to solve real-world problems such as combating climate change.

Autonomous Bus Project

I was the lead of the perception team that developed the software for detection, classification, tracking, and trajectory prediction of dynamic obstacles for a self-driving bus.

Donkeycar @ NUS

This project introduced high school students to the field of robotics through hands-on learning of electronics, Python, and Deep Learning.

Autonomous Underground Train Tracks Inspection Robot

This robot performed safety inspections of tunnels and tracks for underground trains.

The Hydrone Project

Hydrone is a UAV developed for water environments by a team of multidisciplinary students in NUS.

Soft Robotics Catheter

This project aimed to develop an automated catheter to assist surgeons during non-invasive surgeries such as heart operations.

Engineering Good Student Chapter

Under the Technology Development Programme, we developed a system for wheelchairs to overcome the stairs in front of older public housing flats.

HackUST (HKUST Hackathon)

Solving the excessive queuing problem at female toilets with screen displays indicating queues at nearby toilets.

Electric Bicycle Generator Exhibition

A multidisciplinary team of undergraduate and postgraduate students to simulate electricity generation from exercise bicycles.

Redbull Land Rover

This project retrofited a land rover for mobile DJ performances by incorporating a stage and other performance equipment.

Concrete Air Cannon

This is a pressurised air cannon for stress-testing concrete blocks.

Project Nancy

Minimalist user interface on laptops that are designed for elderly users.

Adipose Stem Cells Harvesting

HEAL Hackathon
